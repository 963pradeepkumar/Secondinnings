About the Book

A cricket pitch. A corporate mirror.
A story that resonates.

What begins as casual weekend cricket in a gated community slowly transforms into something far deeper — a team called Awesome Bros, shaped by the same forces that define every workplace: personality clashes, unsung heroes, the ones who always show up, and the ones who quietly carry everyone else.

Second Innings draws vivid parallels between weekend cricket and corporate life. Written with clarity, warmth, and zero jargon, it distils complex leadership lessons into real, relatable moments. You can finish it in a single cab ride — but the lessons stay long after.

About the Author

Pradeep Kumar

Senior Engineering Manager | SaaS & Cloud

Pradeep Kumar is a Bangalorean at heart, an IT professional by career, and a team builder by instinct. With more than two decades of experience in the corporate world, he has led teams across diverse functions, always believing that people come before performance.

Though never a habitual reader or writer, the journey of building Awesome Bros — a weekend cricket team that became much more than sport — inspired him to put his reflections into words for the first time. Through this book, Pradeep shares his belief that leadership is less about titles and more about trust, consistency, and belonging.

This is his debut work, written not from theory, but from lived experience.
